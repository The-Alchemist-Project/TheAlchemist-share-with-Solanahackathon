  • 51% Attack

    A 51% attack (or majority attack) refers to a potential attack on the integrity of a blockchain system

  • Absolute Advantage

    An economics concept in which one party has a direct advantage in efficiency in producing/providing a specific good or service over another party.

    Active Management

    Active management (or active investing) refers to a strategy deployed by fund managers or brokers where they trade financial assets aiming to profit from both bull and bear markets.

    Airdrop

    The term "Airdrop" refers to the distribution of digital assets to the public, either by virtue of holding a certain other token or simply by virtue of being an active wallet address on a particular blockchain.

  • Bear Market

    The term bear market refers to a negative trend in the prices of a market.
